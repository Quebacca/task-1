Overview of Dance

Dancing

Dance is my favourite hobby and one of the things in life that I thoroughly enjoy. Dance isn't also just about going through movements, it provides important life lessons like resilience and time management.

The Genre's of Dance

There are many genre's in dance. These are the ones that my studio focuses on. Ballet: Ballet is mandatory for our studio as it is essential for strength, flexibility, and discipline. It also correlates into other genre's, which is why it is so important. Contemporary: Contemporary mainly focuses on going off of your center. There are no limitations to how you move, but generally, it is just moving freely with a theme or idea. Jazz: Jazz is all about being powerful. We focus on kicks, turns, and leaps, which help build strength and benefit other styles. Tap: Tap is actually my favorite because I am very good at it, and it sounds very nice. You just tap with your feet and create rhythms with your tap shoes. Definitely recommend it. Musical Theatre: We usually focus on building characters in Musical Theatre. The dancing is very easy, but the characters make it challenging. Tumbling: Tumbling is where we learn to do flips and other similar movements, essential for other genres as well.
